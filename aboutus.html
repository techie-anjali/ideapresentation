Present View

We have linked our biogas project with all kinds of manures, fertilizers, and compost that are completely natural and freshly prepared on our farm. Additionally, we have integrated our dairy industry with traditional practices and modern equipment.

Future Vision

We aspire to fully develop this industry and make it accessible door-to-door. Our future plans include creating an application for remote monitoring of the entire farm and expanding our team. We also aim to venture into organic farming.
